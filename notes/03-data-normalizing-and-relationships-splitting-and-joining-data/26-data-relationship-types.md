# Data relationship types

Different kinds of data relationships

- **One-to-Many** (1:n): On record in table A has one or many related record in table B. Example: an employe belongs to one company but a company has many employes
- **Many-to-Many** (n:n): One record in table A has one or many relateed tables in table B - and vice versa. Example: an emplyoes is part of multiaple projects and every projects has multiaple employees assigned to it
- **One-to-One** (1:1): One Record in table A belongs to exactly one record in table B - and vice versa. Example: Employe has exactly one intranet account and every intrenet account belongsto exactly one employee
