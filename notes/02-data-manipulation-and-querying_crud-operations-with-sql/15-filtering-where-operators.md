# Filtering where operators

```sql
WHERE
```

- `=, IS`: Check for equality
  - `=` for most cases
  - `IS` for comparison to `NULL` (and other)

- `<>, !=, IS NOT`: Check for inequality
  - `!=` is alias for "<>"
  - `IS NOT` for comparison to `NULL` (and other)

- `> / >=`: Check for greater than / greater than or equal
- `< / <=`: Check for smaller than / smaller than or equal

- `AND, OR` Combin multiple conditions
